## Email Templates

### OTP Email
```
Subject: Your Abuja Eats verification code

Hi {{name or "there"}},

Your sign-in code is:

{{OTP_CODE}}

This code expires in 10 minutes. If you didn't request this, you can safely ignore this email.

— The Abuja Eats team
```

### Business Claim Approved Email
```
Subject: Your listing claim has been approved

Hi {{name}},

Great news — your claim for {{Business Name}} has been verified.

You can now log in to your business dashboard to update your listing, respond to reviews, and track your profile stats.

[Access your dashboard →]

If you have any questions, just reply to this email.

— The Abuja Eats team
```

### Business Claim Rejected Email
```
Subject: About your listing claim for {{Business Name}}

Hi {{name}},

We weren't able to verify your claim for {{Business Name}} with the information provided.

If you believe this is a mistake, reply to this email with additional proof (e.g. a utility bill, social media link, or CAC certificate) and we'll take another look.

— The Abuja Eats team
```

### New Review Notification (to business owner)
```
Subject: New review for {{Business Name}}

{{Reviewer Name}} just left a {{n}}-star review for your listing.

"{{review excerpt, max 100 chars}}"

[View and respond →]

— Abuja Eats
```
