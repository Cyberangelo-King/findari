# Findari — Brand Identity

> Previously known as Abuja Eats. The brand is warm, local, and trustworthy — built to feel like it belongs to Abuja, not adapted from a global template.

---

## Brand Name

**Findari**
- Working name: Abuja Eats (may be retained for marketing and domain)
- Tagline options:
  - *"Know before you go."*
  - *"Honest reviews, real Abuja spots."*
  - *"Find your next favourite spot."*

---

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Expression |
|---|---|
| Local | Neighbourhood-first navigation, Nigerian Naira, Abuja slang in copy where natural |
| Trustworthy | Verified reviewer system, no anonymous reviews, date-stamped reviews |
| Warm | Orange-red palette, friendly copy tone, community feel |
| Honest | User-powered, unsponsored reviews at the core (paid placement = separate, labelled) |
| Modern | Clean UI, fast performance — not a government portal, not a WhatsApp screenshot |

## Logo

**Mark:** Bowl + spoon icon (`ti-bowl-spoon` from Tabler Icons, or custom SVG)
**Wordmark:** "Abuja Eats" or "Findari" in Inter/DM Sans, 500 weight
**Color on dark (nav bar):** White
**Color on light:** `#E85C2C`
**Minimum size:** 24px height
**Clear space:** Equal to the height of the letter "A" on all sides

### Logo Lockups
- Full: icon + wordmark (horizontal)
- Icon only: square format for favicon and app icon
- Do not: rotate, recolor outside brand palette, place on busy backgrounds

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Direct, warm, conversational — like a friend who knows all the good spots
- Nigerian without being forced — natural references, not caricature
- Never corporate, never vague, never preachy

### Examples

| Context | ❌ Don't | ✅ Do |
|---|---|---|
| Empty state (no reviews) | "No reviews have been submitted for this business." | "No reviews yet — be the first to tell us what it's like." |
| OTP sent | "A verification code has been dispatched to your email." | "We sent a 6-digit code to your email. Check your inbox." |
| Review posted | "Your review has been successfully submitted." | "Your review is live! Other Abuja foodies will see it now." |
| Business claim | "Your claim is under review by our moderation team." | "Got it — we're checking your details. We'll get back to you within 24 hours." |
| Flagged review | "This content is under review." | "We've flagged this review and will look into it shortly." |

## Photography & Image Direction

- **Real food, real people, real Abuja** — no stock photos of plates that don't exist in Nigeria
- **Warm, well-lit** — natural light or warm tungsten; no cold blue-tinted food photography
- **No filters** — clarity and authenticity over Instagram aesthetics
- **Diversity** — Abuja is mixed; photography should reflect the city's actual demographics
- **User-generated photos are the product** — design should celebrate UGC, not suppress it

---

## What Findari Is Not

- Not a delivery app — no cart, no checkout, no ETA countdown
- Not a global template — "neighborhood" is spelled "neighbourhood"; currency is ₦ always
- Not pay-to-win — paid promotions, when they launch, must be clearly labelled
- Not anonymous — trust is built on verified identity, not open-door posting
