# Findari — Goals Document

> Business goals, product goals, user goals, and long-term vision. This document defines what success looks like at every level.

---

## Mission

To give every Abuja resident the honest, reliable information they need to make a good food decision — and give every Abuja food business the platform they deserve to be discovered.

---

## Business Goals

### Short-Term (0–6 months)
- Seed the database with 100+ Abuja restaurant listings before public launch
- Achieve 500 unique visitors in the first 30 days
- Get 20 restaurants to claim their listings within 90 days
- Generate 200 user-written reviews within 60 days of launch
- Establish a 30%+ 2-week return visit rate

### Medium-Term (6–18 months)
- Become the #1 Google search result for "Abuja restaurants review"
- Expand to 500+ active restaurant listings
- Build a community of 5,000+ registered reviewers in Abuja
- Launch paid promoted listings as primary revenue stream (target: ₦500k/month)
- Achieve word-of-mouth growth driven by shared reviews on social platforms

### Long-Term (18 months+)
- Expand platform to Lagos, then Port Harcourt, Kano, and Ibadan
- Become the definitive Nigerian restaurant review platform
- Explore data licensing (anonymised dining trend data) as secondary revenue
- Evaluate native iOS/Android app based on traction and user demand
- Potential: a yearly "Best of Abuja Eats" award to drive PR and engagement

---

## Product Goals

### Trust
- Every review on the platform is by a verified real person — no bots, no anonymous posts
- Review dates are always visible — freshness is a core trust signal
- Flagged content is reviewed within 48 hours
- Business responses are clearly differentiated from reviewer content

### Discoverability
- Any Abuja food spot should be findable in under 15 seconds
- Neighbourhood-first browsing reflects how Abuja residents actually think about the city
- Search works even with partial or colloquial names ("mama put jabi", "the suya place wuse")

### Quality of Information
- Price ranges are always in Naira and always current (updated by verified owners)
- Open Now status is calculated, not estimated
- Photos are real customer photos, not professionally staged marketing shots
- Ratings are broken down (Food / Service / Ambience) — not just a single averaged score

### Experience
- Platform feels fast — 3 seconds max load on Nigerian mobile data
- Mobile experience is primary — not a desktop product ported to mobile
- Writing a review takes less than 2 minutes from tap to submission

## User Goals

### For the Diner (Reviewer)

| Goal | How Findari Delivers |
|---|---|
| "I want to know if this restaurant is good before I go." | Verified reviews with dates, photo evidence, and star breakdowns |
| "I want to know if they're open right now." | Live Open Now badge calculated from business hours |
| "I want to know how much it will cost." | Price range in Naira on every listing, every search result |
| "I want to find food spots near me in Abuja." | Neighbourhood-first search; Google Maps on every profile |
| "I want to leave a review after a bad/good experience." | Simple, fast review flow; verified badge to give your voice credibility |
| "I want to remember spots I want to try." | Save / bookmark feature on every listing |

### For the Business Owner

| Goal | How Findari Delivers |
|---|---|
| "I want new customers to find me." | Free listing visible to all Abuja food searchers |
| "I want to control my information." | Claimed dashboard to update hours, price, photos, description |
| "I want to respond to unfair reviews." | Public owner response on any review |
| "I want to know how my reputation is trending." | Dashboard with avg rating, review count, profile views |
| "I want to be seen above my competition." | Paid promoted listing (Phase 2) |

## What Good Looks Like (90-Day Benchmark)

| Signal | Target | How Measured |
|---|---|---|
| Organic discovery | Users finding Findari via Google, not just social | Google Search Console |
| Review quality | Average review length > 50 words | Database query |
| Return rate | 30%+ of users return within 14 days | Mixpanel |
| Photo uploads | 60%+ of reviews include at least 1 photo | Database query |
| Business engagement | 20+ claimed listings | Database count |
| Trust signal | < 2% of reviews flagged as suspicious | Moderation queue |

## Anti-Goals (What We Will Not Do)

- We will not compromise on review verification to grow faster
- We will not launch outside Abuja until Abuja is genuinely well-served
- We will not add delivery, booking, or payment features in v1
- We will not show undisclosed sponsored content mixed into organic results
- We will not sell user data or profiles to restaurants or third parties
- We will not build for desktop-first — ever
